# AI Hero Academy — Course Content

This folder contains the **actual curriculum content** for the app, organized by track and lesson.

## Tracks
1. `foundations/` — AI fundamentals for beginners.
2. `prompt-engineering/` — practical prompting patterns and evaluation.
3. `build-with-apis/` — building real AI apps with model APIs.
4. `responsible-ai/` — safety, bias, privacy, and governance.
5. `capstone/` — ship a production-ready project.

## Suggested Delivery Format Per Lesson
- **Learning objectives**
- **Concept brief** (5–10 min)
- **Hands-on activity**
- **Quiz checks**
- **Artifact to submit**

## Completion Criteria
A learner is considered job-ready for junior AI builder workflows when they can:
- Build and demo at least one end-to-end AI workflow.
- Evaluate output quality and document tradeoffs.
- Apply responsible AI guardrails in design and deployment.
