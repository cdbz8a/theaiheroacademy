# Track 4: Responsible AI (1 week)

## Outcomes
- Identify common AI risks (bias, privacy, over-reliance).
- Add practical safeguards to prompts and product UX.
- Document human-in-the-loop decisions.

## Modules

### Module 1 — Risk Landscape
- Hallucinations
- Harmful content
- Data leakage
- Automation bias

### Module 2 — Mitigations
- Prompt safety constraints
- Moderation steps
- Sensitive-data redaction
- Escalation policies

### Module 3 — Governance in Practice
- What to log vs not log
- User transparency requirements
- Audit trails and incident response

## Assessment
- Risk register for learner's capstone app
- Responsible AI checklist signed by peer reviewer
