- AI is fast pattern generation
- Great for drafts, summaries, options
- Not automatically accurate
- Not a replacement for accountability

# Slide 8 — Human + AI Partnership Model
1. Human sets context + goals + constraints
2. AI generates options quickly
3. Human verifies, edits, and decides

# Slide 9 — Hallucinations: The Big Risk
- Specific claims with no sources
- Fake citations
- Overconfident tone on uncertain topics
- Internal contradictions

# Slide 10 — Verification Workflow
- Ask for sources
- Cross-check critical claims
- Compare with second tool/source
- Trust but verify for high-stakes tasks

# Slide 11 — Responsible AI Use
- You are the author
- Never paste sensitive/client/company secrets
- Follow policy + compliance
- Disclose AI use when required

# Slide 12 — Tool Landscape: What to Use When
- ChatGPT: general productivity + writing

- Gemini: Google ecosystem workflows

- Perplexity: research + citations
