## Quick Reference — Starter Prompts

### 1) Professional Email Reply
"You are my professional assistant. Draft a reply to the email below. Keep it under 140 words, clear and friendly. Ask 1–2 clarifying questions if needed.\n\n[PASTE EMAIL]"

### 2) Meeting Minutes from Rough Notes
"You are an executive assistant. Convert these notes into: (1) summary, (2) decisions, (3) action items with owner + due date, (4) open questions.\n\n[PASTE NOTES]"

### 3) 2-Week Project Plan
"You are a project manager. Goal: [GOAL]. Constraints: [TIME/BUDGET/TEAM]. Build a 2-week day-by-day plan with risks and a short status template."

### 4) Compare Options
"You are a researcher. Compare [A], [B], [C] with pros/cons and recommendation. Cite sources if browsing is enabled."

### 5) Rewrite in My Voice
"You are my editor. Use this style sample: [SAMPLE]. Rewrite the draft below in the same voice. Keep it human and concise.\n\n[DRAFT]"
