# AI Hero Academy — 11 Module Course Outline (ZIP Source Edition)

Source basis: `imports/files3/` PDFs only.
Duration target: 9–10 hours, hands-on.

## Course Promise
Transform everyday professionals into confident, practical AI users through guided instruction, real task labs, and responsible usage habits.

## Module 0 — Welcome & Community Task Sharing (20 min)
- Introductions and expectations
- Collect top time-consuming tasks
- Define personal goals for the day

## Module 1 — AI Wake-Up Call (40–45 min)
- METR research framing (capabilities doubling trend)
- Why urgency matters now
- AI adoption mindset shift for everyday workers

## Module 2 — The AI Revolution: Origin Story (30 min)
- ELIZA vs modern AI capability comparison
- Why current generation AI feels different
- Human + AI partnership model

## Module 3 — Understanding AI Fundamentals (40 min)
- What AI does well vs poorly
- Pattern prediction vs true understanding
- Practical capability boundaries

## Module 4 — Responsibility & Ethics (45 min)
- Privacy rules and sensitive data boundaries
- Bias, misuse, and accountability
- Human-in-the-loop principles

## Module 5 — Choosing the Right AI Tools (40 min)
- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot by use case
- Task-to-tool matching
- Workflow fit and constraints

## Module 6 — Prompt Engineering Mastery (60 min)
- 5-element prompting structure
- Draft → critique → refine loop
- Reusable prompt patterns for daily work

## Module 7 — Productivity Superpowers (70 min)
- Email drafting and refinement
- Meeting prep, minutes, and action tracking
- Report drafting and structured outputs

## Module 8 — Creative Work & Voice Preservation (35 min)
- Ideation with AI
- Editing to keep authentic tone
- Avoiding generic AI voice

## Module 9 — Advanced Preview (30 min)
- Practical next-level use cases
- Multi-step workflows and role-specific applications
- Growth roadmap for continued learning

## Module 10 — Safety, Control, and 30-Day Capstone (60 min)
- Hallucination spotting and verification checks
- Over-reliance prevention habits
- Personal 30-day implementation plan with measurable actions

---

## Final Deliverables for Students
- Completed workbook activities
- Starter prompt library
- Verification checklist habit
- Personal 30-day plan
