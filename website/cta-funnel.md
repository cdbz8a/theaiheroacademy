# AI Hero Academy — CTA Funnel (Acquisition to Enrollment)

## Funnel Objective
Move prospects from awareness to enrollment with clear next steps and low friction.

---

## Stage 1: Awareness
### Entry Points
- Organic social posts
- Partner/referral mentions
- Direct outreach
- Short educational clips

### CTA
**Get the Free AI Starter Kit** (`/ai-starter-kit`)

---

## Stage 2: Lead Capture
### Landing Offer
Free toolkit includes:
- Prompt starter pack
- Tool selection cheat sheet
- Privacy + verification mini-checklist

### CTA
**Send Me the Starter Kit**

### Thank You Page CTA
**Save Your Seat for the Next Workshop**

---

## Stage 3: Nurture
### Delivery Sequence
- Email 1: Toolkit delivery + quick win
- Email 2: Common mistakes + fixes
- Email 3: Case example + outcomes
- Email 4: Workshop invitation + urgency

### CTA
**View Workshop Details** (`/workshop-preview`)

---

## Stage 4: Consideration
### Workshop Preview Page
- What’s covered
- Who it’s for
- Outcomes and deliverables
- Social proof
- Pricing options

### CTA
**Enroll Now** / **Book Team Session**

---

## Stage 5: Conversion
### Enrollment Page
- Offer summary
- Bonus stack
- FAQ + objections handling
- Deadline / seat cap
- Checkout action

### CTA
**Complete Enrollment**

---

## Stage 6: Post-Conversion
### Thank You / Onboarding
- Confirmation message
- What to prepare
- Calendar add button
- Pre-work prompt (top 3 time-wasters)

### CTA
**Complete Pre-Workshop Prep**

---

## Core CTA Copy Bank
- Enroll Now
- Reserve My Seat
- Get the AI Starter Kit

- See Upcoming Dates
- Request Enterprise Proposal
