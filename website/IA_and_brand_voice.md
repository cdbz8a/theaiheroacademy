---

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- Practical
- Clear
- Confident
- Human-first
- Responsible

### Writing Principles
1. **Specific > abstract** (show concrete outcomes)
2. **Action-oriented language** (learn, apply, ship, save)
3. **No fear hype** (avoid doom and overclaims)
4. **No jargon overload** (plain language for non-technical learners)
5. **Always include guardrails** (privacy + verification)

### Do Say
- "Build real AI workflows for your actual tasks."
- "Use AI safely, with human judgment in charge."
- "Leave with a 30-day rollout plan."

### Don’t Say
- "AI will replace everyone."
- "Guaranteed instant transformation."
- "Fully automated with no human review needed."

---

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Immediate utility** — use AI on day one
2. **Measurable productivity** — save hours weekly
3. **Quality + voice preservation** — better output, still sounds like you
4. **Responsible execution** — privacy-aware, verified outputs
